Oven-Baked French Bread Pizzas

French bread is turned into pizza! Pizza has never been so easy to make! Use your favorite pizza toppings!

Recipe from there

Yield: 6 to 8 servings Prepare time: 10 mins Total time: 25 mins
    Ingredients
  1. 1 loaf French Bread
  2. Pizza sauce homemade or store bought
  3. Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  4. Your favorite pizza toppings-Josh used sausage caramelized onions, and roasted red peppers this time

    Instructions
  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  • Slice French bread loaf in half lengthwise and place on a large baking sheet. Evenly spread pizza sauce over the bread. Top with cheese and desired toppings.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cheese is melted. Cut and serve!
  • Note-you can cut the French bread into 4 pieces. This is fun for a pizza party. Everyone can make their own pizza with their favorite toppings!
Enjoy your meal!
